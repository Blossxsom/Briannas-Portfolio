Pencil/infosys App Challenge

For this challenge I worked with a team to create mood appeal. High school students and young adults often neglect their needs due to academic stress and various responsibilities, leading to mood swings and identity crises. It's crucial to not only enhance their mood but also ensure lasting improvements for their emotional growth. Mood Appeal is our app that recommends foods based on your mood. It helps users find mood-enhancing foods, offers personalized suggestions, and includes journaling features to better understand and respond to their emotions.
